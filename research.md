# Research Interests

My current interests center on affective computing and intent recognition.
This page can be expanded into a more detailed research statement.

## Affective Computing

Understanding, modeling, and evaluating emotional or affective signals in
human behavior and interaction.

## Intent Recognition

Inferring user goals, communicative intent, or behavioral intention from
multimodal or contextual signals.

## Human-Centered AI

Designing AI systems that are interpretable, useful, and aligned with human
needs in real-world settings.
